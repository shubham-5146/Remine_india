A Technology that reaches a 95% Revaluation ratio



REMINE_INDIA recycling has developed an efficient & cost effective process for recycling lithium ion battries.
the most widely used batteries for electrical vehicles and portable electronics today.

This new process will allow upto 95% of battery components to be recovered & treated so that they
can be reused by battery manufacturers - concrete way to close the loop of battery life cycle.

The technology put forward by REMINE_INDIA recycling uses an inovation combination of processes based on
hydrometallurgy. The result is a size and chemistry agnostic process emitting very low GHG emissions.
